Strawberry Microgreens Salad

2 cups mixed microgreens (such as arugula, radish, and pea shoots)

1 cup strawberries, sliced

1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese

1/4 cup chopped walnuts or pecans

2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar

1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil

1 teaspoon honey

Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Directions

Step 1

In a large bowl, combine the mixed microgreens, sliced strawberries, crumbled feta cheese, and chopped nuts.

Step 2

In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, and honey until well combined. Season with a pinch of salt and a dash of black pepper.

Step 3

Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat the ingredients evenly.

Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
